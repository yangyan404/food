    We were there on Sunday around 5pm for late lunch / early dinner and this place was quite empty. We were seated outside as we asked for smoking area. Unfortunately the heater lamp was out of gas, so that we need to bear the windy day. Anyway it was fine. Funny thing was those plates they use to serve the food reminds me of our primary school's time... not that I'm complaining tho. The one impressed me the most was the black pepper burger. Mac n cheese were just normal, Chocolate milkshake was just so so (not that special as those review), Bacon n cheese was fine, P.I.G as those pulled pork style was fine. But the black pepper was the bomb! well done 70% was perfect for my taste bud as the burger itself still has the distinct reddish color and was very juicy. Black pepper was strong and seriously suits my tastebud. The fat-fries served on the sides added additional point to perfection! The next time I visit this place again, I'd probably order the same!!!
